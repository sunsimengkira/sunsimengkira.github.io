# Next Round Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [DATE]

Next Round is designed to help you track friendly tabs and who is next.

## Tab Data

Your tab information is stored on your device.

Next Round does not upload, sell, rent, or share your tab data.

Next Round is not a banking app, payment app, or debt collection tool. It is a lightweight way to remember friendly tabs between people who trust each other.

## Analytics and Tracking

Next Round does not currently use third-party advertising trackers.

## Purchases

If Next Round offers paid features, purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. Next Round does not receive your full payment card information.
