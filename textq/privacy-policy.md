# TextQ Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [DATE]

TextQ is designed as a local, temporary texting queue.

## Contact Lists

TextQ does not upload, sell, rent, or share your contact list.

Imported contact files are processed on your device. TextQ does not create a permanent cloud contact database.

## Messages

TextQ uses Apple’s Messages compose screen to help you review and send each message yourself.

TextQ does not automatically send messages and does not access the delivery status of messages after they are sent through Apple Messages.

## Image Attachments

If you attach an image to a message, the image is used only for the message you choose to prepare and send.

## Analytics and Tracking

TextQ does not currently use third-party advertising trackers.

## Purchases

If TextQ offers paid features, purchases are handled by Apple through the App Store. TextQ does not receive your full payment card information.
